Questions, answered

Frequently asked

What is Hound's Hearth?
Hound's Hearth is an independent pet-wellness brand offering premium nutrition, supplements, treats and grooming — with honest guidance and real support for our partners.
What is pawTree?
pawTree is our product supplier and partner platform. They manufacture and ship the products, run the ecommerce and customer service, and track and pay partner commissions.
Why do I register on pawTree?
Because pawTree powers fulfillment, ecommerce and commission payments. You enroll through pawTree under the Hound's Hearth organization — which connects you to both the established pawTree platform and our partner community.
Who pays commissions?
pawTree tracks and pays your commissions directly through their official partner platform, on their schedule.
Do I need to hold inventory?
No. pawTree handles products, shipping and billing. You share and recommend; they fulfill and ship.
How much can I earn?
pawTree pays up to 45% on a new customer's first order and up to 30% on reorders. Rates are set by pawTree and can change, and there are no income guarantees — your results depend on your own effort.
Who is this program for?
Groomers, breeders, trainers, influencers, pet businesses, and any dog lover with an audience or community who values quality.
Can I promote online and offline?
Yes — social media, content, in person, or in your salon or business. We provide resources for both, subject to pawTree's partner advertising guidelines.
